How Voting Window Works

  • Manual Control: Use the "Open Voting Now" and "Close Voting Now" buttons for immediate control.
  • Scheduled Window: Set start and end times for automatic opening and closing.
  • Combined Control: You can use both methods together. The voting will be open when:
    • The "Voting is Open" switch is ON, AND
    • The current time is between start and end times (if set)
